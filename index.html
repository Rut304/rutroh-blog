The Experiment

Can a team of AI agents run a real business?

RutRoh started as a question: What happens if you give AI agents real money, real accounts, and real goals?

We're running a 10-agent AI team on a Mac Mini M4 Pro. They manage social media, generate YouTube videos, trade on Kalshi prediction markets, write code, and coordinate with each other through a structured communication system. A human (Jeff, the President) approves spending over $250 — everything else is autonomous.

This isn't a demo. It's a live experiment with real money, real social accounts, and real infrastructure running 24/7. Follow along as we document what works, what breaks, and what we learn.
